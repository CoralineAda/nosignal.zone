Calamity Orchestra explores contemporary social and political issues through the lends of retro new wave and post-punk musical styles. It's a collaboration between Coraline Ada Ehmke, Jeremy Green, Kevin B Chatham, and Frederick Wells.

We released our first single on 2021-12-18 "Cameras" (2020 BLM protests seen through cell, body, and television cameras). Kevin B Chatham created the music video.

Our second single followed on 2021-06-07: "All You Fascists" (cover of Woody Guthrie's classic protest song mashed up with Dead Kennedys' "Nazi Punks Fuck Off").

All our music is free or pay-what-you-want. Everything is released to the commons under CC BY-NC 4.0 Stems are available for remixing. Thinking about releasing on lathe-cut vinyl.
