Music Projects

  • Calamity Orchestra

    Calamity Orchestra explores contemporary social and political issues through the lends of retro new wave and post-punk musical styles. A musical collaboration between Coraline Ada Ehmke, Jeremy Green, Kevin B Chatham, and Frederick Wells.

    We released our first single on 2021-12-18 "Cameras" (2020 BLM protests seen through cell, body, and television cameras). Kevin B Chatham created the music video.

    Our second single followed on 2021-06-07: "All You Fascists" (cover of Woody Guthrie's classic protest song mashed up with Dead Kennedys' "Nazi Punks Fuck Off").

    All our music is free or pay-what-you-want. Everything is released to the commons under CC BY-NC 4.0 Stems are available for remixing. Thinking about releasing on lathe-cut vinyl.

  • Shapes with Two Edges

    A collaboration with synth pop duo Eloquent. They keep giving me major keys, I keep reaching for harmonic minors. Randall Scott creates most of the synth tracks, so I mainly focus on guitar, (spending a lot of time with an ebow. An experiment to see what happens when crisp, precise, crystalline digital meets messy, analog, and atmospheric. The name comes from an obscure Euclidean degenerate.

    We've got plans for a 4-song LP, digital and vinyl. They're all works in progress.
