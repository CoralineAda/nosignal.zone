Watch the official video for the first Calamity Orchestra single, "Cameras". The song is a reflection on how the world viewed the anti-racist and anti-fascist protests in 2020, mediated by cameras-- cell, body, media.

The video for our second single, "All You Fascists (Fuck Off)", should be ready sometime in August. In the mean time, you can download both singles on Bandcamp.
