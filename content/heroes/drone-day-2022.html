For the second year in a row, on 2022-05-09 I took part in Drone Day, a 24-hour, global celebration of drone & experimental music.

This year's piece is called "The Goth", featuring samples from the Gay Byrne interview with 19-year-old goth Siobhan. You can listen to the WIP on soundcloud.

This is part of a 5-year project tentatively called "Irish TV", featuring a series of 5 monotonic compositions inspired by a 1983 Irish TV talk show. Every successive Drone Day, I will live-stream while I compose, record, and produce a new song in the series.

The complete Drone Day 2022 live session is available on YouTube.
