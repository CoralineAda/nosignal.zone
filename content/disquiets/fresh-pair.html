My second contribution to Disquiet Junto, responding to the prompt "A Fresh Pair".

The prompt invited combining two disparate cultural influences. I decided to go with musical influences, so I paired the Gary Numan of 1978 with the Gary Numan of 2018, creating "Splintered Android".

Headphones are recommended, and please remember to be kind to your electric friends.

Listen to "Splintered Android" by Coraline Ada Ehmke on Soundcloud, and see how many references you can catch.
