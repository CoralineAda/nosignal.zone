For the second year in a row, on 2022-05-09 I took part in Drone Day, a 24-hour, global celebration of drone & experimental music.

This year's piece is called "The Goth", featuring samples from the Gay Byrne interview with 19-year-old goth Siobhan. You can listen to the WIP on soundcloud.

This is part of a 5-year project called "Gangs of Ireland", featuring a series of 5 monotonic compositions inspired by a 1983 Irish TV interview show. Every successive Drone Day, I will live-stream while I compose, record, and produce a new song in the series.

The complete Drone Day 2022 live session is available on YouTube.

The studio version of this track is available on the Gangs of Ireland playlist on soundcloud.
