This is a living room recording (one mic, in the middle of the room) of my old band from Austin, Uncle Karma, jamming and improvising with some friend-of-a-friend horn players. We only have the vaguest memory of this happening. It was recorded in Austin, Texas on August 4, 1993.

My friend, former bandmate, and most recently Auntie Kismet collaborator rescued five selections from the session from on an old cassette tape he found.

Come feel that 90s freeform jazz vibe with "Uncle Karma With Horns (August 4, 1993)".
