A Little Fire Scarecrow was a recording project featuring percussion by Jeremy Green and contributions from Kevin Chatham (bass), Frederick Wells (bass), and Alva Snædís (guitar and bass).

It was my return to music after thinking I had lost my creative center. It was an important part of my journey, not only musical but also personal.

4 years, 8 albums, 65 songs. Only 8 songs left standing. RIP 2016-2020
