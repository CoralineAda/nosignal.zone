I got my very first music review on 2021-12-14, on the Hard Times 'Fanboys' Podcast! Starting around the 27:23 mark, they talk about "The Punk", my experimental Drone Day piece.

  • "It made me feel a way that drone music never has, that was really really cool"
  • "It could be in the soundtrack for Prometheus"
  • "It's creepy, it's haunted, but also a little bit uplifting. You feel wonder."

I can't thank The Hard Times enough for the kind words and constructive feedback. Listen to the song "The Punk" on Soundcloud.
