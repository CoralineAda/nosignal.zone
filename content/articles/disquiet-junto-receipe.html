Disquiet Junto 0633, "Voice Swap", suggested recreating an existing piece using my voice. The easiest way to do this, of course, was to travel back in time and become my own producer.

The original piece, "(It Begins With) Unrest", opened the last album by A Little Fire Scarecrow and also opened my weekly studio stream on twitch during the plague years.

BE ADVISED: You haven't heard a backward drum hit until you've heard it with headphones on.
