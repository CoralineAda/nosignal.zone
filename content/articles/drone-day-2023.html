2023 marked the 3rd year of my 5-year "Gangs of Ireland" project. Starting in 2021, each year on Drone Day (droneday.org) I've created a new experimental piece inspired by a segment from an 80s-era Irish talk show featuring young goths, punks, & mods. This year's piece is "The Iconoclast".

Check out my Drone Day 2023 performance video from a live performance at an experimental music event in Hudson Valley, New York.

The studio version of this track is available on the "Gangs of Ireland" playlist on SoundCloud.
